About Pinterest

Pinterest is a place to discover and save ideas for whatever you're into — recipes, home projects, style, and everything in between.

This project is a Django learning app inspired by Pinterest. It's built to explore how a real photo-sharing feed, user accounts, boards and saved pins work together, from the database models up to the front end.

Our mission is simple: help people find inspiration and turn ideas into action.
